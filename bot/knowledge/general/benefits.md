# Employee Benefits

## Health Insurance
All employees are covered by a comprehensive health insurance plan starting from day 1. The plan covers:
- General practitioner visits
- Specialist consultations
- Dental (up to $500/year)
- Vision (up to $300/year)
- Emergency care

Family members can be added to the plan at a subsidised rate.

## Meal Allowance
Employees receive a monthly meal allowance of $100 credited to their corporate card, usable at partner restaurants and canteens.

## Transportation
A monthly transportation allowance of $80 is provided. Alternatively, company shuttles run from 3 central city locations.

## Gym & Wellness
The company reimburses up to $600/year for gym memberships, fitness classes, or wellness apps. Submit receipts via the HR portal.

## Learning & Development
- Annual learning budget of $1,500 per employee for courses, conferences, and books.
- Internal training library with 500+ courses available.
- Study leave of up to 5 days/year for external certifications.

## Equipment
All employees receive a laptop (MacBook Pro 14" or equivalent), external monitor, keyboard, and mouse. Home office equipment can be requested for remote workers.

## Mental Health
Free access to 6 sessions per year with a certified therapist through our EAP (Employee Assistance Program). Completely confidential.

## Stock Options (ESOP)
Eligible employees (after 1 year) receive stock options vesting over 4 years with a 1-year cliff.

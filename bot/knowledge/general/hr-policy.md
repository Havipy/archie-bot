# HR Policy

## Working Hours
The standard working week is 40 hours, Monday to Friday, 9:00–18:00. Flexible start times (8:00–10:00) are available with manager approval.

## Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work requests must be submitted at least 2 days in advance via the HR portal.

## Annual Leave
- Full-time employees receive 28 calendar days of paid annual leave per year.
- Leave must be planned and approved at least 2 weeks in advance.
- Up to 14 days of unused leave can be carried over to the next year.

## Sick Leave
Sick leave is covered by the company for up to 30 days per year. Extended illness is covered by the national social insurance fund. A medical certificate must be submitted within 5 working days.

## Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually (June and December). Employees receive written feedback and discuss goals with their line manager.

## Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to act professionally, respectfully, and in accordance with company values. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.

## Onboarding
New employees complete a 2-week onboarding program that includes introductory sessions, tool access setup, and meetings with key stakeholders.
